    Toni Iuruc (born in 1979) is a Romanian Businessman and Sportsperson Partner from Macedonia. He is well-identified as the longtime lover of Simona Halep (Romanian professional tennis player).

    She ranked as the world No. 1 player in singles twice between 2017 and 2019. Furthermore, Simona also remained No. 1 for 64 weeks. Later, Halep created history after she ranks tenth in the Women’s Tennis Association (WTA) rankings.

    In her successful career, the player won 19 WTA singles titles. In fact, she also finished runner-up 17 times. Simona even bagged two Grand Slam singles titles: the 2018 French Open and the 2019 Wimbledon Championships.

    The tennis player won her first six WTA titles in the year 2013. In fact, she also became the first one to do so since Steffi Graf (German former professional tennis player) in 1986.

    Halep bagged titles like WTA Most Improved Player, 2018 French Open, 2019 Wimbledon Championships, WTA Most Popular Player of the Year, WTA Fan Favorite Singles Player of the Year, Patriarchal Cross of Romania, the Order of the Star of Romania, and much more.
