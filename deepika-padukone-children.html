    Deepika Padukone children-Famous Indian actress, Deepika Padukone was born on January 5th, 1986 in Copenhagen in Denmark.

    She was born to Prakash Padukone and Ujjala Padukone. She shares the same parents with her younger sister, Anisha Padukone who happens to be a golfer.

    She received her education at Sophia High School in Bangalore and finished her pre-university coursework at Mount Carmel College.

    She then registered for a sociology bachelor’s degree program at the Indira Gandhi National Open University, but she eventually withdrew owing to scheduling issues with her modeling job.

    According to Padukone, she did not have many friends growing up and was socially uncomfortable. Her life was b adminton, which she played competitively since she was a small child. Throughout her time at school, Padukone 

    continued to play badminton and pursue a profession in it. She also competed in national competitions. She participated in a few state-level baseball events as well.

    While focusing on her studies and athletic endeavors, Padukone also worked as a child model, making her debut in a few ad campaigns at the age of eight. She altered her direction in the tenth grade and resolved to become a fashion model.

    She started working as a model full-time in 2004 under the guidance of Prasad Bidapa. In the beginning of her career, Padukone became well-known thanks to a television advertisement for the soap Liril. She also modeled for numerous other companies and goods.

    She made her runway debut for designer Suneet Varma at the Lakme Fashion Week in 2005, and she also took home the “Model of the Year” honor at the Kingfisher Fashion Awards.

    Padukone moved to Mumbai at the age of 21 and stayed at her aunt’s house. She became more well-known that year after appearing in the “Naam Hai Tera” music video by Himesh Reshammiya.

    Soon, Padukone started to get offers for movie roles. Since she didn’t think she had enough acting experience, she enrolled in a course at Anupam Kher’s film school.

    After much media speculation, Om Shanti Om director Farah Khan decided to cast her for a role after spotting her in Reshammiya’s song video. She also attributes getting the part to the assistance of fashion designer Wendell Rodricks. Malaika Arora was contacted by Farah Khan while she was looking for a model to star in her upcoming movie.

    Padukone is one of India’s highest-paid actresses and has won three Filmfare Awards, among other honors. She is listed among the country’s most well-known public figures; Time named her one of the world’s 100 most important people in 2018 and gave her the TIME100 Impact Award in 2022.

    She competed in badminton at the national level as a youngster, but she gave up the sport to pursue a career as a model. She quickly started receiving offers for movie roles, and in 2006, she made her acting debut as the lead in the Kannada movie Aishwarya.

    Then, in her first Bollywood film, the love story Om Shanti Om (2007), Padukone co-starred with Shah Rukh Khan in a dual role, earning her the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut.

    After receiving plaudits for her lead part in the 2009 romantic comedy Love Aaj Kal, Padukone experienced a momentary setback.

    Her career took off after she landed a lead role in the romantic comedies Cocktail (2012) and Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani (2013), the heist comedy Happy New Year (2014), Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s historical dramas Bajirao Mastani (2015) and Padmaavat (2018), and the Hollywood action movie XXX: Return of Xander Cage (2017).

    She also won two Filmfare Awards for Best Actress for her performances as Juliet-inspired heroine in Bhansali’s tragic romance Goliyon Ki Raasleela Ram-Leela (2013) and as a bossy architect in the comedy-drama Piku (2015).

    In 2018, she established her own business, Ka Productions, through which she produced and appeared in the commercially unsuccessful films 83 in 2021 and Chhapaak in 2020.

    The Live Love Laugh Foundation, which raises awareness of mental health in India, was founded by Padukone. She is outspoken on topics like feminism and depression, performs on stage, has written newspaper articles, created her own line of women’s clothes, and is a well-known celebrity ambassador for goods and brands.

    Does Deepika Padukone have kids?

    Padukone has no child with her husband, Ranveer Singh. Padukone has said in this statement that as of now she is not yet ready to become a mother and neither is her husband Ranveer Singh ready to become a father because both want to focus more on their career.
