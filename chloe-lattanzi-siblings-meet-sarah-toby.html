    Biography of Chloe Lattanzi

    In January 1986, Chloe Rose Lattanzi was born in Los Angeles, California. She is the great-granddaughter of Nobel Prize-winning physicist Max Born and the daughter of actress and singer Olivia Newton-John and actor Matt Lattanzi.

    In 1994’s A Christmas Romance, 1998’s The Christmas Angel: A Tale on Ice, and 2001’s The Wilde Girls, Lattanzi played the lead role. She made an appearance in a Paradise Beach episode from 1993. She was a cast member of the MTV reality show Rock the Cradle and frequently made appearances on the TV show Entertainment Tonight.

    Chloe was a finalist for a Young Artist Award in 2002 for her work on The Wilde Girls. Her first record as a singer, “Wings And A Gun,” was published in 2010, and she collaborated with her mother Olivia Newton-John and Dave Aude on the single “You Have to Believe” in 2015.

    Chloe Lattanzi has two siblings, Sister Sarah Newton-John; Brother Toby Newton-John.
