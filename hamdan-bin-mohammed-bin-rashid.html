    Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid is the Crown Prince of Dubai, UAE, with a net worth of $400 million. Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid was born in Zabeel Palace, Dubai, United Arab Emirates, in November 1982. He publishes poems under the moniker Fazza.

    Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid was crowned Crown Prince of Dubai on February 1, 2008. He is the son of Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum and Sheikh Hind Bint Maktoum bin Juma Al Maktoum.

    Who is Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid?

    Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid is the son of Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum and Sheikha Hind bint Maktoum bin Juma Al Maktoum, Mohammed’s senior wife. He is the second of their twelve children and the fourth of his father’s children; his father has three sons. Sheikh Rashid bin Mohammed was Hamdan’s oldest full brother.

    Hamdan bin Mohammed attended the Rashid School for Boys in Dubai before moving on to the Dubai School of Government. He continued his education in the United Kingdom, graduating from Sandhurst in 2001 and later attending the London School of Economics.

    In his interview with Vision, he described how Sandhurst taught him the value of self-discipline, commitment, virtue, responsibility, perseverance, understanding, teamwork, camaraderie, and the advantages of hard work.

    How old is Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid?

    He is currently 41 years old.

    What is Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid net worth?

    The renowned Prince is estimated to be worth $400 million.

    What is Hamdan bin Mohammed bin Rashid career?

    Hamdan bin Mohammed was named Chairman of the Dubai Executive Council in September 2006. On February 1, 2008, he was appointed Crown Prince of Dubai, while his brother Sheikh Maktoum bin Mohammed Al Maktoum became Deputy Ruler of Dubai.

    As the new hereditary prince, Maktoum hired new important people and financial advisors, including economist John Calverly and hedge fund figure James T. Naeem, while he also became the head of HN Capital LLP. He is the director of the Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Establishment for Young Entrepreneurs, and he serves on the Dubai Sports Council and the Dubai Autism Center.

    He was a member of the Dubai World Expo 2020 delegation when the emirate won the privilege to host the event. A few days after winning World Expo 2020, he ascended to the top floor of the Burj Khalifa and waved the UAE flag. He is the originator of the Hamdan International Photography Award, which began in 2011.

    In June 2022, Sheikh Hamdan unveiled the “Dubai Global” plan, which aimed to construct 50 commercial representative offices for Dubai across five continents. The project aimed to support Dubai-based businesses and boost the city’s reputation as a business hub.
