    Months after the two denied reports that they were romantically linked, dating rumors about Kim Kardashian and Tom Brady have once again resurfaced.

    The two were among the many celebrity guests at billionaire Michael Rubin’s annual White Party in the Hamptons on July 4 (which also saw the appearance of stars like Beyoncé and Jay-Z, Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck, Leonardo DiCaprio, Kendall Jenner, and many others).

    An insider told DailyMail.com that Kardashian and Brady seemed to get cozy with each other during the festivities. “Kim and Tom were super flirty with each other at Rubin’s party and were seen during the day on the beach together and again dancing at night,” the source told the outlet, adding that the NFL player is “exactly [Kim’s] type.”

    Getty Images

    Back in May, multiple outlets reported that Kardashian and Brady may have embarked on a new romance, with the Skims mogul allegedly considering buying property in the Bahamas near Brady’s vacation home. However, those rumors were quickly squashed.

    Brady’s reps denied the rumors to Entertainment Tonight, while another source told the outlet that the duo are “just friends.” The source added, “Kim and Tom have friends and business partners in common, but they are not dating.”

    Brady announced his split from supermodel Gisele Bündchen last October. Meanwhile, Kardashian finalized her divorce from Kanye West late last year.

    Kardashian—who dated Pete Davidson for about nine months after her split from West—has hinted at her dating life during the ongoing third season of Hulu’s The Kardashians.

    Reflecting on how her relationship with Davidson has made her prioritize privacy in her new romance, she said, “You obviously learn from every situation, and the one thing I learned from [the] last situation was the media made me feel like I was in a very serious relationship so quickly. I just want to sneak around a little bit. There’s nothing wrong with wanting to sneak!”
