    David Beckham facts

  • He was born at Whipps Cross University Hospital in Leytonstone, London.
  • He missed the 2010 World Cup due to injury.
  • He is the English record holder for an outfield player. When he played against Slovakia in March 2009, he beat Bobby Moore’s 108 caps.
  • David scored his first international goal against Colombia on 26 June 1998 at the 1998 FIFA World Cup at the Stade Félix Bollaert, Lens.
  • In 2005 he opened the David Beckham Academy football school in London and Los Angeles, but closed it again in 2009. Instead, he developed a mobile academy for traveling around the globe.
  • Beckham suffers from obsessive-compulsive disorder. Because of this, he uses three different refrigerators for drinks, salads, and groceries.
  • He suffers from ornithophobia, the fear of birds.
  • He has been a supporter of UNICEF since his days at Manchester United.
