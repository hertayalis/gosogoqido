    Early Life

    MissKirRoyal Andrea was born on the 23rd of January, 1987 in Sofia, Bulgaria.

    Career

    MissKirRoyal Andrea, after graduating from high school, signed a recording deal with Bulgarian record label Payner Music. She then met Romanian singer Costi Ionita, and together they produced the track, Samo Moi.

    Since their combination was much loved and brought more success, they became one of the Balkans’ most famous duos.

    The following year after the duo was formed, they came up with Sahara. The single shoot them to even more popularity as they progressed as one of the most loved duos in Bulgaria.

    In 2009, not to make it seem like they were relaxing, MissKirRoyal Andrea and Costi Ionita released yet another talked about single, Tyalee. The single was released in 2009 and featured the talented musical artist, Lenox Brown.

    Due to her performance with the Romanian singer Costi Ionita on Sahara and other songs, in 2013, BBC and Payam Forghani described her as a Pop-folk Superstar.
