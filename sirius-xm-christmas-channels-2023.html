    SiriusXM Christmas stations: When can I listen?

    SIRIUSXM is continuing its festive tradition by offering subscribers a host of Christmas stations. As the 2023 holiday season approaches, fans of the subscription service want to know how to tune in to its Christmas radio stations.

    09 Jun 2024, 00:00

    SIRIUSXM is continuing its festive tradition by offering subscribers a host of Christmas stations.

    As the 2023 holiday season approaches, fans of the subscription service want to know how to tune in to its Christmas radio stations.

    What are SiriusXM's holiday stations?

    SiriusXM offers multiple holiday stations, including:

    • Holly - "Contemporary holiday hits"
    • Holiday Traditions - "Traditional holiday recordings"
    • Hallmark Channel Radio - "Timeless Christmas music and carols"
    • Holiday Soul - "Classic soul and Motown holiday music"
    • Country Christmas - "Country Christmas music that spans generations"
    • Christmas Spirit - "Ad-free Christmas music and holiday favorites exclusively from contemporary Christian artists" 
    • Holiday Pops - "Classical Christmas carols and holiday favorites performed by the greatest classical artists of all-time"
    • Acoustic Christmas -"New and classic holiday songs from well-known singer-songwriters and bands"
    • Mannheim Steamroller Channel - "Celebrate the holidays with Chip Davis of Mannheim Steamroller featuring their signature blend of symphonic, new age and rock inspired Christmas music"
    • Navidad - "Latin holiday music and traditional sounds"
    • Jolly Christmas - "Upbeat, energetic holiday hits"
    • Holiday Instrumentals -"String, piano, and orchestra versions and more of your favorite holiday hits"
    • Noël Incontournable - "Francophone Christmas classics and contemporary holiday hits from the ‘60s to today"
    • Jazz Holidays - "Christmas favorites from classic legends and contemporary jazz stars alike"
    • '70s /'80s Holidays -"The biggest and most familiar Christmas and holiday songs from the 1970s and ‘80s"
    • Rockin' Xmas - "Classic Christmas songs that rock, including hits, rarities, live tracks, and humorous tunes"
    • Holiday Chill-Out - "Chill, downtempo holiday music"

    When do the SiriusXM holiday channels launch this year?

    This year, the SiriusXM holiday channels mainly launched in November 2023.

    However, this varies based on the specific channel.

    The launch dates for each station include:

    • Holly - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and Satellite Channel 105 
    • Holiday Traditions - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and Satellite Channel 71
    • Hallmark Channel Radio - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and on Satellite Channel 104 from November 23 through December 26
    • Holiday Soul - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and on Satellite Channel 49 from December 7 through December 26
    • Country Christmas - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and on Satellite channel 58 from December 7 through December 26
    • Christmas Spirit - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and on Satellite Channel 63 from December 7 through December 26
    • Holiday Pops - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and on Satellite Channel 76 from December 24 through December 25
    • Acoustic Christmas - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App and on Satellite Channel 6 from December 24 through December 25
    • Mannheim Steamroller Channel - November 1 through December 26, 2023 on the SXM App
    • Navidad - November 1 through January 6, 2024, on the SXP App
    • Jolly Christmas - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App
    • Holiday Instrumentals - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App
    • Noël Incontournable - Year-round on the SXM App
    • Jazz Holidays - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App
    • '70s /'80s Holidays -November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App
    • Rockin' Xmas - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App
    • Holiday Chill-Out - November 1 through December 26, 2023, on the SXM App

    How much does SiriusXM cost?

    According to its website, SiriusXM offers three "popular plans," with different pricing, including:

    • Music Showcase - Three months for $1, then $13.99 per month
    • Music & Entertainment - Three months for $1, then $18.99 per month
    • Platinum - Three months for $1, then $23.99 per month

    The Music Showcase plan includes more than 100 channels total, while the Music & Entertainment plan includes over 400 total channels.

    The Platinum plan offers subscribers more than 425 total channels.
