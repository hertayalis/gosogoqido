    In Skyrim, there are several characters who can become your follower and join you on your adventures. These followers can provide assistance in battles, carry items for you, and offer their unique skills and abilities. Here are some of the characters that can become your followers in Skyrim:

    1. Aela the Huntress

    Aela the Huntress is a member of The Companions guild and can become your follower once you’ve finished the questline. She can be found in the bar of Markarth.

    2. Farkas

    Farkas is another member of The Companions guild and can become your follower after completing the questline. He can be found in the bar of Markarth.

    3. Athis

    Athis is one of the members of The Companions guild and can become your follower once you’ve finished the questline. He can be found in the bar of Markarth.

    4. Njada Stonearm

    Njada Stonearm is a member of The Companions guild and can become your follower after completing the questline. She can be found in the bar of Markarth.

    5. Torvar

    Torvar is another member of The Companions guild and can become your follower once you’ve finished the questline. He can be found in the bar of Markarth.

    6. Ria

    Ria is a member of The Companions guild and can become your follower after completing the questline. She can be found in the bar of Markarth.

    7. Vilkas

    Vilkas is a member of The Companions guild and can become your follower once you’ve finished the questline. He can be found in the bar of Markarth.

    8. Serana

    Serana is a character introduced in the Dawnguard DLC and can become your follower during the questline. She is a vampire and has unique abilities. You can find her in Dimhollow Crypt.

    9. Cicero

    Cicero is another possible follower and can be recruited during the Dark Brotherhood questline. He can be found at the Falkreath Sanctuary.

    10. Barbas

    Barbas is a talking dog and can become your follower during the quest “A Daedra’s Best Friend”. He can be found near the Falkreath Hold.

    11. J’zargo

    J’zargo is a Khajiit mage and can become your follower once you complete his personal quest. You can find him at the College of Winterhold.

    12. Mjoll The Lioness

    Mjoll The Lioness can become your follower after completing a small task for her. She can be found in Riften.
